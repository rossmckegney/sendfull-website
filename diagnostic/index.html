The Autonomy Decision Matrix

Two questions decide it: could you, and should you.

The Automation Strategy Gap

The distance between automation aspiration and execution reality.

Organizations launch into automation reactively — chasing a vision of full autonomy without first asking whether they could deliver it, whether they should, and whether the organization can absorb it. That gap is where initiatives quietly drift, budgets burn, and quality slips. The diagnostic measures how exposed you are.
